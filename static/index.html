About HeartAI

HeartAI is an explainable artificial intelligence platform built to support early cardiovascular risk screening. It combines a deep learning classifier with a feature-contribution analysis so every prediction comes with a transparent, human-readable explanation rather than a black-box number.

Features

🧠 Deep Learning

🔍 Explainable AI
